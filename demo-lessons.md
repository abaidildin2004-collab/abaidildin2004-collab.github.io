## Sample Demo Lesson Ideas

The examples below are placeholders that can later be replaced with your actual lesson plans, slides, recordings, or teaching reflections.

### Lesson 1: Introduction to Critical Reading

- Objective: Help students identify main ideas and supporting details
- Activity: Read a short passage and discuss key points in pairs
- Outcome: Students summarize the text in their own words

### Lesson 2: Vocabulary Through Context

- Objective: Develop vocabulary using real examples from a text
- Activity: Students infer word meaning before confirming with class discussion
- Outcome: Learners practice using new words in short sentences

### Lesson 3: Interactive Speaking Practice

- Objective: Improve confidence in spoken communication
- Activity: Small-group discussion with teacher prompts
- Outcome: Students express opinions clearly and respectfully
