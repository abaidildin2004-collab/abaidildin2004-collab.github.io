## About Me

My teaching philosophy centers on clarity, patience, and meaningful connection. I aim to help learners build confidence while developing practical knowledge and independent thinking skills.

- Clear communication and consistency
- Lifelong learning and continuous improvement
- Encouraging curiosity and creativity

## Career Goal

I hope to contribute to a school community where I can support student development, collaborate with colleagues, and continue growing as an educator.
